About Al-Hikma Hospital

Excellence in healthcare since 1998

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Al-Hikma Hospital has been at the forefront of medical excellence for over two decades. What began as a small community hospital has grown into a comprehensive medical center serving thousands of patients annually.

Our commitment to innovation, compassionate care, and medical excellence has earned us recognition as one of the region's leading healthcare institutions. We continuously invest in cutting-edge technology and ongoing education to ensure our patients receive the highest quality care.

Today, Al-Hikma Hospital stands as a beacon of hope and healing, with state-of-the-art facilities, world-class medical professionals, and a unwavering dedication to improving the health and well-being of our community.
